Instructions

Welcome to the InterviewPrep Hackathon app! Here are some instructions to get you started:

  • Select the role you want to practice from the dropdown menu.
  • Click the "Start" button to begin the interview session.
  • Click the "Speak" button to start speaking and the "Stop" button to stop.
  • Use the "End" button to end the session and get feedback.
  • Hover over the "Instructions" button to view these instructions.
